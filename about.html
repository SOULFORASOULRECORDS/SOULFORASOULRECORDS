Who We Are and What We Do

We are NOT a normal label. If you’re expecting money handed to you or instant growth, you should look elsewhere.

We, as a team and family, help each other grow together. In SOULFORASOULRECORDS, we expect professionalism and respect.

Run by Tarell Hinds, the founder (and also an artist), he has found that many artists work against each other instead of understanding that everyone can work together to grow.

SOULFORASOULRECORDS is not for one specific genre. Artists from all genres and people of all backgrounds, regardless of skin color, are welcome here.

Unlike most record labels, you’re not obligated to share money, and we will NOT take money from you. We and other members work together, sharing trade secrets and social media strategies with one another.

YOU OWE US NOTHING.
